About PhonoMack

PhonoMack is a music site. It was originally planned to launch 4 years ago but due to funding issues we weren't able to begin working on the project and we truly apologise for the delay. Since the beginning we planned on making our website as user friendly as possible, filled with song suggestions from a variety of categories to choose from. As of today, there are improvements made to the site, with new songs added every week. Currently we are developing sound products for you to become the next, best music producer. This includes, mixing consoles with the latest of technology on-board, microphones, speakers, LDC headphones and many more. PhonoMack is a website dedicated to music lovers. Our original idea was to provide the user with a player to play their local file. As time passed by the idea of having a website doing that would be tons of times better. In partnership with Spotify we assure you of high quality music and a plethora of categories to choose from.
